About Sip Savvy

Welcome to Sip Savvy, your personal guide to the world of delicious beverages. Whether you're a coffee connoisseur, a tea enthusiast, or just looking for a refreshing new drink, our collection has something for everyone.

Our mission is to make it easy and fun to discover and create amazing drinks at home. You can browse, filter, and search through our ever-growing library of recipes. Found something you love? Add it to your favorites to easily find it again later!

This project was built with a passion for good drinks and clean code. We hope you enjoy using it as much as we enjoyed building it.

Cheers!
